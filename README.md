This repository is ancillary to the paper <br>
"The Diagrammar of Quantum Magnusian" <br>
by Li Guo, Joon-Hwi Kim, Jung-Wook Kim, Sungsoo Kim, Sangmin Lee, Jian-Rong Li <br>
 <br>
We provide a Mathematica notebook "Users.nb” accompanied by folders containing data files. <br>
 <br>
For the current version, the data covers the following cases. <br>
V = 2, L = 0 <br>
V = 3, L = 0, 1 <br>
V = 4, L = 0, 1, 2, 3 <br>
V = 5, L = 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 <br>
V = 6, L = 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 <br>
V = 7, L = 0, 1, 2 <br>
 <br>
We focus on "primary" graphs with no "banana loops". <br>
Up to V = 5, our data exhausts all possible values of L of a primary graph. <br>
For V = 6, 7, due to limited computational resources, we stop at low values of L. <br>
 <br>
In the BW basis, we suppress the cut propagators, as they do not directly affect Murua coefficients. <br>
Then, it suffices to consider L = even. <br>
 <br>
The notebook “Users.nb” has two main features. <br>
a. Retrieve the Murua coefficient of a given graph. <br>
b. Illustrate how the Murua formula works for a given graph. <br> 
Examples are given in Chapter 4 of the notebook. <br>
 <br>
A known Issue. <br>
Banana loops may not be correctly displayed if you are using Mathematica version lower than 12.1.
